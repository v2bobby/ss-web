THE LITERACY-MECHANISM GAP

Public health fails when
anatomy is invisible.

Patients receive labels like “hypertension” without understanding the vascular resistance or cardiac load driving their condition. This gap is not academic — it is lethal.

Low Treatment Adherence

Patients are far less likely to follow protocols they do not mechanically understand. Knowledge without context breeds disengagement.

Up to 50% of chronic disease patients discontinue care

Myth Proliferation

In the absence of clear anatomical facts, misinformation fills the void — detox teas, miracle cures, and dangerous folk remedies spread unchecked.

Health myths spread 6× faster than facts on digital platforms

Health Inequity

Low-bandwidth communities lack access to clear, medically accurate, jargon-free anatomical education. The result is a widening knowledge divide.

Global disparities in health literacy remain stubbornly high
